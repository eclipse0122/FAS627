New Qi Settlement Walkthrough

Choose Gunpowder, Ropery & Beekeeper for goods

Choose Light, Ranged & Artillery for military units

 

 

        Initial Setup:

v Move Town Hall to upper right corner

v Choose expansions with no impediments to save shards

Build (as shown in photos, if possible):

 

1.     Build 9 Breweries, 2 Palaces, 1 Church, 6 Estate Houses, 1 Multistorey House

2.     Buy one expansion (100 shards)

3.     Build Gunpowder Manufactory

4.     Wait one hour until goods building is ready

5.     Produce 12 goods & sell the goods building afterwards

6.     Buy another expansion with 30 goods

7.     Build 1 Palace and 3 Multistorey Houses

 

 

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       First Collection:

1.     Collect three Multistorey houses & sell them

2.     Collect all remaining buildings

3.     Sell the Church

4.     Build two Gallows

5.     Build three Estate Houses

 

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Second Collection:

1.     Collect all buildings

2.     Sell one Palace

3.     Build one Spice Trader & complete instantly (25 shards)

4.     Build one Gallows & two Multistorey houses

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Third Collection:

1.     Collect from one MultiStorey House then sell it

2.     Collect all other buildings

3.     Build one Pillory

4.     Sell one Palace

5.     Build a Beekeeper

6.     Wait one hour then produce 40 goods & sell Beekeeper

7.     Buy one expansion with 60 goods

8.     Build four MultiStorey Houses, a Ropery & Two Estate Houses

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Fourth Collection:

1.     Collect from two MultiStorey Houses then sell them

2.     Collect all other buildings

3.     Produce 70 rope

4.     Buy one expansion with 90 goods

5.     Build a Catapult Camp, produce 20 catapults, then sell it (optional step)

6.     Build 2 Alchemist & 2 Estate Houses

 

 

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Fifth Collection:

1.     Collect from one MultiStorey House then sell it

2.     Collect all other buildings

3.     Build one Cartographer & one Frame House

 

 

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Sixth Collection:

1.     Collect all buildings

2.     Sell the Palace

3.     Build a Cooperage & complete instantly (50 shards)

 

 

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Seventh Collection:

1.     Collect all buildings

2.     Sell one MultiStorey House

3.     Build a Clapboard House & complete instantly (50 shards)

4.     Sell one Brewery

5.     Build an Alchemist

Wait for next collection (fight battles in the meantime)

 

       Eighth Collection:

1.     Collect all buildings

2.     Sell one Brewery

3.     Build a Cooperage & complete instantly (50 shards)

4.     Sell two MultiStorey Houses

5.     Build two Frame Houses

6.     Produce enough Rope (130) & buy another expansion

 

 

*Recommend switching to farming at this point (optional)

Wait for next collection

 

       Ninth Collection:

1.     Collect all buildings

2.     Build a Cooperage

3.     Build two Pillories

4.     Sell one Brewery

5.     Build an Alchemist

 

 

Wait for next collection (produce Rope for map donations)
